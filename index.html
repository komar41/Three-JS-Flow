Flow Visualization with Three.js and d3
Instructions
  • Press and hold left click on the mouse and drag left, right, up or down to rotate the cylinder.
  • Use the trackpad to zoom in/zoom out.
  • The particles colored according to their concentration value.
  • Use the slider to move the plane along Z-axis and filter particles according to the Z-value.
  • Click on "Color All Particles" button to show all particles.
